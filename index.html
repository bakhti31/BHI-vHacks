What is Crew

A Crew allows several players to join and work together.
Every crew can have a maximum of 20 members comprising the Leader, Co-Leader(s) and Members.
The crew news tab can be seen by everyone in the crew, and can be edited by Leaders.
Both the Leader and Co-Leaders is able to invite, kick, promote or demote members in the crew.
Creating a crew costs 8k vTCs and you must provide a unique name and tag. To join a crew, you have to be invited by a Leader or Co-Leader of the crew through the Messenger application using the "/invite" command.

What Crew Can Do?

Rush Tournament

The Rush tournament is a feature available within the Crew Application. The goal is for each member to upgrade the target software as much as possible within the 24 hour time span. For every level upgraded of the target software your crew will gain 1 point. The total rewards that are listed on the Rush tab are divided among all crew members based on how much they contributed to the total score (if you contributed to 50% of the total score you get 50% of the total reward).

Money Transfer Tournament

The Money tournament is a feature available within the Crew Application. The goal is for each member to hack as much money as possible with bank transfer and TR. transfer within 30 minutes. There is a limit of $500B for each hack, and you have the take the maximum amount (do not move the slider) from bank transfers for it to count as a valid hack. The tournament appears 4 times a day. Check the Tournament tab for more information when tournamnets start. The total rewards that are listed on the Money tab are divided among all crew members based on how much they contributed to the total score (if you contributed to 50% of the total score you get 50% of the total reward).

Deface Tournament

Implemented on 9/4/2021 on Android and 10/4/2021 on iOS for vHack Revolutions. Version 1.1.80 Deface consist in being part of a crew. Each Deface has a cooldown of 30 minutes, in which will appear your crew's logo in victim's log. To apply a Deface you'll need the same amount or more of the victims antivirus (AV) in malware kit (MWK). A Deface can not be erased from any log during 30 minutes since you apply a Deface to your victim's log. Scores: Each Deface you apply on anyone gives automatically 50 points to your crew in Deface Tournament. Anyone who exploits and see your crew's deface it grants to your crew another 10 points for every player who saws it. Note : On the opposite of Trojan Transfers, Defaces only accepts MWK, making SDK useless for defaces. You can only have 1 Deface active in those 30 minutes, which nobody can change it, erase it or delete it.

Rank Weekly Tournament

Weekly tournament it's the total score from money tournaments and rush tournaments together from the week, every place in those tournaments will give some score to the weekly tourney. The rewards will be splited for 20 members, every member will receive the same reward.
